Case Studies — Anthology Inc.
Case study 01
100+ WBT Course Library for Anthology Student
One course per feature — complete self-service training for every product capability
Storyline 360Rise 360CaptivateSCORMNorthpass
The challenge
Anthology Student is a feature-rich SIS platform. Customers — university administrators and staff — needed self-paced training to independently learn every feature without relying on live support or costly training sessions.
My approach
Architected a WBT library where each course covered exactly one product feature. Every module: feature context → step-by-step software simulation of the live environment → guided practice → knowledge check. Used Storyline 360 and Captivate for interactive simulations, Rise 360 for structured overviews.
Design decisions
One feature = one course for clarity. Simulations mirrored the real Anthology Student environment exactly. SCORM-packaged for LMS deployment, tracking, and completion reporting.
Example — "How to Generate an Academic Records Report"
The WBT shows exact steps in a simulated environment: open Anthology Student → navigate to Reports module → select Academic Records → configure filters (student ID, academic term, programme) → preview the report → generate and export as PDF. On-screen click indicators, callout boxes, and narration guide every action.

The challenge

Produced 50+ screen-recorded training videos in Camtasia showing the exact Anthology Student environment. AI-generated voiceover (ElevenLabs) narrated each step. Trint auto-generated accurate captions and transcripts for WCAG 2.1 accessibility compliance.
Production quality
Callout annotations and zoom-in effects highlighted key clicks. Consistent branding across all 50+ videos. Videos embedded inside WBT courses and published standalone on Moodle for quick reference.

Case study 03
AI-Accelerated Production Pipeline at Scale
Delivering 150+ assets — WBTs and videos — without sacrificing quality
ElevenLabsTrintStoryline 360CamtasiaAgile
The challenge
Building 100+ WBT courses and 50+ Camtasia videos simultaneously is a massive undertaking. Traditional voiceover recording and transcription workflows were creating bottlenecks — especially when product updates required rapid course revisions.
My approach
Designed a repeatable AI-first production workflow: scripts written per module → AI voiceover (ElevenLabs) → automated captions (Trint) → Camtasia recording with callouts → packaged in Storyline/Captivate with knowledge checks → SCORM-deployed to LMS.
Team impact
Pioneered this workflow within the Anthology L&D team. The pipeline became a reusable template for all new feature training — meaning when Anthology Student launched new features, new courses and videos could be produced rapidly.
